Beaches

Calabria is known for its beautiful beaches – from long sandy stretches to pebbly coves. It has around 500 miles of coastline, which takes in all the geographical variations of the Tyrrhenian and Ionian seas. Next to Scalea's beach is a variety of restaurants and even an internet cafe, so you can make sure you are connected.

Mountains

Everywhere you look, you are surrounded by mountains. Nothing beats waking up in the morning and sipping a cup of freshly brewed coffee whilst gazing out onto the rolling hills and peaks. If you listen closely, you can hear the jangle of the bells around the goats’ necks as they graze along the grassy mountainsides.
